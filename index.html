What is AirFlow?

Air pollution is a deadly, man-made problem, responsible for the early deaths of some 7,000,000 people every year, around 600,000 of whom are children. It is estimated that 90 per cent of the world’s population breathe polluted air.*

*Source: United Nations

But what can we do against it? What can be done to reduce air pollution for citizens from Stuttgart to Shanghai immediately and in the long run?

Our Answer: The intelligent gathering and use of air pollution data for …

Citizens - especially the most vulnerable (i.e. children, elderly)
A mobile App to find the lowest Air-Pollution routes from A to B. → Immediate reduction of the exposure to air pollution
Cities / Communities
High-Resolution, real time mapping and forecasts of air-pollution → Empower communities to take action for the reduction of overall air pollution

Our Approach: The use of artificial intelligence to combine data from stationary and mobile sensors, satellites and weather stations. For its outstanding concept, the AirFlow-Team was awarded a Microsoft AI for Earth Grant.
