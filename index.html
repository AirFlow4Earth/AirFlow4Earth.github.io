What is AirFlow?

Our responsibility, ownership and commitment is looking towards the customers satisfaction by our creativity and involvement to serve by identifying sources and scores of the flow of air-pollution to reduce our chronical illness risks of human beings. Especially for Our youngest and oldest people Engaged breathing sportive people like biker or jogger. Further on for Pedestrians beside the traffic of vehicles People living besides the roads Passengers in each vehicle.

Our big question: How to move from A to B at the inner part city of Berlin?

We seek map in real-time - by AI based scenarios - the dynamic of pollution data from satellites, local vehicles and stations. TOP for the health of each human mover is the lowest pollution-score for his walk/drive for example: particulate matter, NOx, CO², temperature, wind direction and knots.

What we do about it?

route planning (before traveling), route guidance (live)
