Mission

We know that technology can help people to adapt with changing air conditions.

We are a start-up with the goal of mitigating health risks associated with air pollution. By combining data sets, we integrate known relationships from research journals to advise a diverse set of users on reasonable routes to avoid unnecessary exposure to pollution. Our AI makes pollution foreseeable and improves health based decision making.

Influencing climate adaptive behaviors

Individuals with health conditions affected by pollution can lead healthier lives with slight changes to the routes that they walk, run, or exercise.

Improving long distance trekking performance

Endurance athletes, medium to long distance (20 to 100 km) bicyclists, and health conscious people can have increased control over the routes that they choose, so that they can perform during training at their best without being concerned over one additional thing hindering their performance.

More reactive and properly coordinated auto air filtration systems

One of the largest cost expenditures in vehicle design is their air filtration systems. With reasonable routing alterations on navigation, beyond speed or distance, the proper coordination of air filtration settings can be suggested to improve indoor cabin air quality, including small adjustments in navigation.

Industry Gaps

Gaps exist within the current market that address air quality route decision making. Current service providers are often stuck selling sensors to augment poor quality data sets and provide instructions that cover a city or a large area that lacks relevance to users. We are able to use our insights gained from literature reviews and awareness of the current state of technology to reach a 5 m2 per 15 minute resolution to inform guidance, possibly better. It is possible to provide reasonable guidance for every journey and exercise.
